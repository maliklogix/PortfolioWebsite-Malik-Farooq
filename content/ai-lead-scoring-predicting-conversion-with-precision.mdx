# AI Lead Scoring: Predicting Conversion with Precision

Not all leads are created equal. In a high-volume marketing environment, the bottleneck is often the sales team's time. **AI Lead Scoring** solves this by using predictive analytics to rank leads based on their likelihood to convert.

## Beyond Firmographics
Traditional lead scoring looks at static data: Job title, Company size, Location.
AI Lead Scoring looks at **Behaviors**:
- How many pages did they visit?
- Which specific whitepapers did they download?
- What was the sentiment of their last support inquiry?
- How does their journey compare to past customers who successfully converted?

## Implementing Predictive Scoring
We build custom ML pipelines that integrate with your CRM. The system analyzes historical data to find hidden indicators of "Buyer Intent."
*example: A lead who visits the pricing page twice and reads three technical case studies in 48 hours is automatically flagged as a "Hot Lead" and assigned to a senior sales rep.*

## Conclusion
Stop wasting your best talent on low-intent cold leads. Use AI to focus your energy where the ROI is highest.
