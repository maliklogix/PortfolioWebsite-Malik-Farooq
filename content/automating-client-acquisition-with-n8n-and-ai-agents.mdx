# Automating Client Acquisition with n8n and AI Agents

At MalikLogix, we believe that the best marketing teams aren't the largest—they are the best automated. Today, we’re sharing the architecture for a "Set and Forget" client acquisition system.

## The Core Stack
- **n8n**: The glue that connects everything.
- **OpenAI / Anthropic**: The "brain" for classification and research.
- **Instantly.ai / Apollo**: The outreach engines.
- **Supabase**: The lead database.

## The Workflow Pattern
1. **Signal Discovery**: Our agent monitors LinkedIn or specific niche forums for people asking questions related to AI automation.
2. **Deep Research**: The agent visits the lead's profile, reads their recent posts, and identifies their specific pain points.
3. **Dynamic Personalization**: Instead of a template, the AI writes a context-aware 2-sentence hook based on the lead's actual activity.
4. **CRM Injection**: The system qualifies the lead based on revenue/industry and adds them to a drip campaign.

## The Result
This system allows a solo founder to perform the outreach and research volume of a 10-person SDR team, with better personalization and zero burnout.

# Conclusion
Automation is no longer about saving time; it's about gaining a competitive edge by doing things at a scale that is impossible for humans.
