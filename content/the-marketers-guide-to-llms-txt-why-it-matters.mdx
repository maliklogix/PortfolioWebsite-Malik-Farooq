# The Marketer's Guide to LLMS.TXT: Why It Matters

As AI tools like Perplexity and ChatGPT Search become the primary way users find information, your website needs to be readable for *machines*, not just humans. Enter **`llms.txt`**.

## What is it?
Similar to `robots.txt`, `llms.txt` is a proposed standard for providing a high-quality, machine-readable summary of your website. It resides in the `public` root and helps LLMs understand your core offerings without getting lost in your HTML.

## Why Marketers Should Care
1. **Accuracy**: By providing an `llms.txt`, you control the narrative. You ensure the AI has the most accurate data about your products, pricing, and services.
2. **Efficiency**: LLMs have context limits. A concise `.txt` file ensures they skip the boilerplate and focus on your value proposition.
3. **Visibility**: Search engines are evolving into "Answer Engines." If the AI can't easily parse your site, you won't be cited in the final answer.

## Implementation Checklist
- A 1-paragraph summary of your brand.
- Direct links to your most important case studies.
- A machine-readable list of your services.
- Frequently Asked Questions summarized for LLMs.

## Summary
The `llms.txt` file is the digital business card for the AI era. If you don't have one, you're invisible to the future of search.
