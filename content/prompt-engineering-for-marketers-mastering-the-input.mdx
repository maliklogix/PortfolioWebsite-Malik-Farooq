# Prompt Engineering for Marketers: Mastering the Input

In the age of AI, your performance as a marketer is directly tied to the quality of your inputs. This is **Prompt Engineering**. It's not magic; it's a predictable science of providing context, constraints, and clarity.

## The Framework: C.O.R.E.
1. **Context**: Give the AI a persona. "You are a world-class copywriter specializing in high-ticket Shopify sales."
2. **Objective**: Be extremely specific about the goal. "Write a 3-sentence hook for a Facebook ad targeting luxury watch collectors."
3. **Reference**: Provide examples. "Use the punchy style seen in the attached brand guidelines."
4. **Execution Rules**: Set hard boundaries. "Do not use emojis. Do not use the word 'excited'. Keep under 50 words."

## Iteration is Key
A prompt is never "done." The best marketers treat prompts like code—they test, debug, and version-control them. At MalikLogix, we maintain a central Prompt Library that is shared across all our automation workflows.

## Summary
AI tools are like high-performance sports cars. Prompt Engineering is the skill of driving them. Master the input, and you’ll master the output.
